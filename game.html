Hints

When the game border is red, it means the bear is close, try to get away!

Make sure your inventory isn't full, otherwise you may miss out on valuable loot!

The sooner you get better equipment, the better.

Help

Use the arrow keys to move your player about the map. You can also use the arrow keys on either side of the player if you prefer.

When you move, the bear also moves. If the bear comes into contact with you, you will die.

You can interact with the environment by pressing enter when you are in the vicinity of an appropriate interact-able environment.

You can mine the environment for items then you can use these items to craft other items - be warned though, you can only carry so much!
